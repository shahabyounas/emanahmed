Research Focus

The Gormley Lab focuses on developing new polymer-based materials for biomedical applications, with particular emphasis on high-throughput approaches for biomaterial discovery.

Key Research Areas

  • Polymer-protein conjugates for therapeutic delivery
  • High-throughput polymer synthesis and screening
  • Machine learning for biomaterial design
  • Enzyme stabilization using synthetic polymers
  • Automated liquid handling for biomaterials research
